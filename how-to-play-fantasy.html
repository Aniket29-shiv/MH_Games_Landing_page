Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to play a fantasy premier league?
    After signing up for the Fantasy Premier League you will be given a certain amount to build a team. You will have to make a squad of 11 and your players will be allotted points in the fantasy game as per their performance in the real-life match.
  • How do you play fantasy sports?
    You have to pick a platform and sport, to begin with. You will have to be aware of all playing conditions, players' form and performances, and points system before starting to play.
  • What are fantasy games or sports?
    In fantasy games or sports, fans build their own imaginary teams based on points that the real-life players generate through their on-field performances. The fans win or lose based on the performances of their selected players and teams in real tournaments.
  • Is fantasy games a skill or luck?
    Fantasy games are skill-based games where a user analyses and creates a team based on the participating teams, ground, and weather.
  • How does the Fantasy Premier League work?
    In Fantasy Premier League players create their own fantasy team and go against other players in the contest leaderboard. The player with the most points on the leaderboard wins cash prizes at the end of the match.
  • How do people earn in fantasy cricket leagues?
    Fans are required to select players to make their playing XI. Each of these players carries points, which go through changes in accordance to their on-field performances. The accumulated points are thus used to determine the overall ranking of a fan's team, which helps in ascertaining how much money a player is going to make.
